Artist Bio

I’m an artist who believes small moments carry big magic. I create tiny, hand-painted watercolor scenes — just 2.5 × 3 inches — set into simple pine frames. These miniature “windows into other worlds” are the pieces available here on my website, created so collectors can bring a bit of that same quiet magic into their own spaces. My work is rooted in wonder, nostalgia, and the gentle beauty hiding in everyday life.

I paint dreamy atmospheres, quiet in-between places, and whimsical mycelium structures that feel like they could be homes, lanterns, or living architecture from another gentle world. Much of what I create is inspired by the act of giving — offering small pieces of beauty out into the world like visual keepsakes. I hope my art feels like a deep breath, a warm drink, or a memory you didn’t know you had.
